Les Crêpes

Description

Crêpes are a wonderful dessert, it is a very popular thing in France, quite a religion in Brittany, a region in the North West of France

It is sooo easy to make, good with children also. You stuff it with what you want

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • A bit of water
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Rum
  • Vanilla

Steps

  1. Mix everything until the batter looks perfect
  2. Let it rest covered in the fridge for 8 hours
  3. Throw some on a very hot pan
  4. enjoy
