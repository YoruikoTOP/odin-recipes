Pizza

Description

Pizza is my favorite thing in life, I'd die for it, really

The dough can be a bit tricky to make but once you master it, you can't go back, it's already too late

Ingredients

  • Flour type 00
  • water
  • Sourdough
  • Salt
  • Tomato
  • Mozzarrella di Buffala for God's sake
  • An oven, yikes

Steps

  1. Mix the Flour, water, salt and sourdough
  2. When the dough looks very smooth let it rest for some time
  3. Make the pizza shape
  4. Throw tomato sauce on it
  5. Bam in the very hot oven
  6. few minutes before it's ready put the Mozza on it
  7. Eat the damn pizza
